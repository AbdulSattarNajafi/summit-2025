About the Sangfor Partner Summit

We are thrilled to invite you to the Sangfor Partner Summit 2025, an event that continues to celebrate the power of partnership and innovation. Just as you've helped Sangfor deliver world-class solutions to customers across the globe, we remain dedicated to equipping you with the most advanced technologies to fuel your continued success.

This year's theme — AI & XaaS for a Better Future — highlights our shared vision of leveraging Artificial Intelligence and Everything-as-a-Service to create smarter, more agile, and more sustainable businesses. Together, we are not just adapting to the future — we are shaping it.

Join Sangfor executives, global partners, and industry leaders for a dynamic program of keynote presentations, expert panels, 1-on-1 partner engagements, and vibrant networking opportunities. We'll also honor your outstanding contributions with the Global Partner Awards, and unveil the strategic roadmaps and innovations that will define our journey forward.

Let's build a better future — powered by AI, enabled by XaaS, and driven by partnership.
